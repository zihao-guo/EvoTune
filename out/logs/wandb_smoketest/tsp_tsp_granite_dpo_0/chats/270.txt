def heuristics(distance_matrix):

The heuristic score for each edge is computed as the product of the flow weight and the sum of out-degrees (incoming flow) and in-degrees (outgoing flow) of the connected nodes. This penalizes edges that have high out-degree (strong sources) and high in-degree (strong sinks), making the function more aware of the flow dynamics in a network.

The negative sign before the product ensures the function tries to minimize these values (make the heuristic score as close to zero as possible), making it a useful heuristic for network design problems.
