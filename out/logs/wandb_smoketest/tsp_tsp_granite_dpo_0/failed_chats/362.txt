A high betweenness centrality score suggests the vertex (edge) is 'central' and more desirable to include in our solution.

    heuristics_matrix = nx.to_numpy_array(G, nodelist=G.nodes(), weight='weight')
    
    # Normalize centrality scores to be between 0 and 1 (optional, depends on specific use case)
    # Here we'll use a simple normalization by dividing each centrality by the max centrality
